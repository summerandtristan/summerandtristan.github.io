How we met

I first met Tristan in in 2010 when I was back home from college for the summer. We had mutual friends in Marietta, but had somehow never really talked before then. We instantly connected over our love for beer, music, and science fiction. At first we thought it was just a summer fling, but I needed someone to help me drive my car out to Colorado and Tristan needed a change of scenery (I actually ended up having a fever after the first day, and Tristan had to drive the last 20 hours on his own)! The rest, as they say, is history. We still live in Boulder, and work in the area as software engineers.

Events

Under the Sun

Cocktail Hour & Appetizers 4PM - 5PM

Arrive at our reception site for refreshments and appetizers. Park in front (SW corner of Broadway and Table Mesa). A shuttle will pick you up to take you to the ceremony site from there. Shuttles will start leaving proptly at 5PM. There is virtually no parking at Sunrise Amphitheater so we will need everyone to be present at Under the Sun before 5PM!

Reception & Dinner 7PM - 2AM

After the ceremony a shuttle will take you back to Under the Sun where we will be serving dinner and dessert. There will be many alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks to choose from as well. Music and dancing to follow. so don't forget your dancing shoes!

Sunrise Amphitheater

Ceremony 6PM - 7PM

The ceremony will take place on top of Flagstaff Mountain with a stunning view over the entire city of Boulder. There is no free parking here and virtually no paid parking either. Please park at Under the Sun and take the shuttle. There is a rustic bathroom near Sunrise Amphitheater, but it is advised to try and go before getting on the shuttle. Due to the nature of this being an outdoor ceremony in May please be prepared for all tipes of weather (sun, rain, snow, wind). Temps could be anywhere between 50 to 80 degrees Fahrenheit.

What to Wear

Outdoor Wedding - Dressy Casual
Wear whatever you want, but come prepared. May in Colorado can mean lovely sun, but it can also bring rain, snow, wind, etc. Temperatures can range from 50 - 80 degrees Fahrenheit, and when the sun sets it drops a good 20 degrees as well. Bring layers if you can. We encourage you to check the weather before packing, and again before the day of the wedding as the forcast can change overnight.
